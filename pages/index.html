The Process

From Prototype to Shelf

Every funded game on TabletopForge follows the same four-stage journey.

01

Campaign Review

Studios bring their game to the Launch page. Our team reviews quality, playability, and market fit before approval.

02

Campaign Launches

The game goes live with a funding target, stretch goals, and pledge tiers. Backers pledge to bring the game into production.

03

Funded & Produced

Successful campaigns go into production. We monitor milestones and keep every backer informed with live updates.

04

Find at Retail

Post-campaign, funded games appear in our retail finder so new fans can buy their copy anywhere in the world.
