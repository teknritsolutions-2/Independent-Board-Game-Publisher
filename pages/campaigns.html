How Pledging Works

Back a campaign in three simple steps. Your pledge is only charged if the campaign meets its funding goal.

01
Discover a Campaign
Browse live and upcoming campaigns. Filter by genre, funding status, or search for something specific. Read the full design story.
02
Choose a Pledge Tier
Each campaign offers reward tiers — from digital rule books to deluxe editions with miniatures. Pick what excites you most.
03
Secure Your Pledge
Enter your details. Your card is only charged if the campaign hits its goal. Full refund guaranteed if it doesn't fund.
04
Track & Receive
Watch the campaign progress, get backer-only updates from the designer, and receive your game when it ships worldwide.
Pledge Protection
Your pledge is 100% refundable if a campaign doesn't reach its funding goal. No risk, no worries.
Verified Designers
Every campaign goes through our vetting process. We review game design, production plans, and creator track record before approval.
Backer Support
Our team mediates between backers and designers to resolve any fulfilment issues. We don't disappear after a campaign ends.
Global Fulfilment
Integrated shipping to 38 countries through our logistics partners. Track your game from warehouse to doorstep.
