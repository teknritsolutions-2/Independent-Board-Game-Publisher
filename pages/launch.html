Process

From Idea to Funded — In 5 Steps

Our process is built to get your campaign in front of the right people as quickly as possible.

01
Apply
Submit your game concept using our application form. Include a prototype video, rulebook, and production plan. Reviews take 5-7 days.
02
Onboard
If approved, you'll be paired with a dedicated campaign manager who guides you through campaign page setup, pledge tiers, and timing.
03
Pre-Launch
Build buzz with a "Coming Soon" listing. Collect wishlists, get featured in our newsletter sent to 47,000+ backers, and prepare your community.
04
Go Live
Campaign launches with full platform support — analytics dashboard, backer messaging, stretch goal management, and real-time notifications.
05
Fulfil
After funding, our logistics partners help you produce and ship to backers in 38 countries. We handle customs, tracking, and backer communication.

Eligibility

Is Your Game Ready to Launch?

We review all applications before approval. Here's what you'll need to submit a strong application.

  • A complete game concept with defined rules
  • A working prototype (digital or physical)
  • A prototype demo video (2–5 minutes)
  • A rulebook (draft is fine)
  • Estimated production cost & timeline
  • A clear funding goal in mind
  • Game suitable for 1–8 players
  • Original IP (no licensed games)

  Don't have everything yet? Contact us — we offer a free pre-application consult for early-stage designers.

FAQ

Designer Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before you apply.

We aim to review all applications within 5-7 business days. If your application is approved, you'll receive an onboarding email from your campaign manager within 24 hours of the decision.
If a campaign doesn't reach its funding goal, all backers receive a full refund automatically. You pay nothing — no platform fees, no listing fees. You're free to re-apply with an updated campaign at any time.
Absolutely. You retain 100% of your intellectual property at all times. TabletopForge is a crowdfunding platform, not a publisher. We never take any rights to your game, artwork, or brand.
After your campaign closes, we connect you with our network of fulfilment partners who handle warehousing, packing, and shipping to 38 countries. We negotiate competitive rates on your behalf. You can also use your own manufacturer and shipper if preferred.
Yes. TabletopForge supports designers from over 30 countries. We handle currency conversion, international payments, and local tax compliance so you can focus on your game. Contact us for country-specific details.
