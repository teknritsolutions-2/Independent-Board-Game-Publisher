Kingdoms of Ash

By Ember & Forge Studio · Fantasy Strategy · 2–5 Players · Ages 14+

A World Consumed by War

Kingdoms of Ash is a dark fantasy area-control board game for 2–5 players. Set in a realm shattered by centuries of magical conflict, players command ancient houses vying for dominion over the ash-covered continent of Vaelthar.

Each house brings a unique playstyle — from the brutal siege tactics of House Morrath to the cunning espionage networks of House Silven. Choose your path, forge alliances, and break them at the right moment.

Key Features

  • Asymmetric factions with unique abilities and win conditions
  • 80+ hand-illustrated event cards and a modular hex map
  • Campaign mode with persistent consequences across 10 scenarios
  • Premium resin miniatures for all faction leaders

When does the game ship?

We estimate fulfilment by Q1 2027. All backers will receive tracking information when their order ships. We'll post regular production updates throughout the process.

Is this a standalone game or an expansion?

Kingdoms of Ash is a fully standalone game. No other purchases are required. The Dragon Lord tier includes an exclusive expansion that adds new mechanics and a 6th faction.

How long does a game take to play?

Average play time is 90–150 minutes depending on player count and experience. The campaign mode scenarios run 60–90 minutes each.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to 38 countries. International shipping costs are calculated at pledge manager stage after the campaign ends. We use regional fulfilment hubs to minimise costs and customs delays.
