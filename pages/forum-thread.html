Anyone else blown away
by Kingdoms of Ash?

by MarcusR·2 hours ago·48 replies
MarcusR
2 hours ago · 248 posts
OP

Just backed the Warlord tier on Kingdoms of Ash and I'm genuinely blown away. The campaign page is so clean, the art direction is incredible, and the asymmetric faction design sounds exactly like what I want from a fantasy area-control game.

Has anyone actually playtested it? Would love to hear firsthand impressions of the mechanics — particularly the espionage system for House Silven.

SanaO
1 hour ago

I playtested it at GenCon last year! The espionage system is genuinely clever — you're placing face-down influence tokens that opponents can't verify. The tension is excellent. House Silven felt like playing a completely different game to the other factions.

TomK
45 min ago

Backed Dragon Lord tier. No hesitation. The miniature quality in the prototype photos is on another level for an indie studio. Ember & Forge clearly know what they're doing.
