Frequently Asked
Questions

For Backers
How does backing a campaign work?

When you back a campaign, your card is charged when the campaign successfully ends (if the funding goal is met). You receive your rewards after the fulfilment process is complete, which is typically 6–18 months after campaign end.

What happens if a campaign doesn't reach its goal?

If a campaign doesn't reach its funding goal, no charge is made to your card. You'll receive an email notification and can explore other campaigns.

Can I change my pledge tier after backing?

Yes, you can upgrade or change your pledge tier any time before the campaign ends. After the campaign, you'll have an opportunity to adjust during the pledge manager phase.

Do you ship internationally?

Each campaign sets its own shipping policy. Most games on TabletopForge ship to 30+ countries. Shipping costs and any applicable customs fees are shown at checkout during the pledge manager phase.

For Designers
How do I submit my game?

Create a free designer account, fill out the submission form with your game concept, target player count, genre, and prototype materials. Our team reviews all submissions within 5 business days.

What percentage does TabletopForge take?

TabletopForge charges a 5% platform fee on successfully funded campaigns, plus standard payment processing fees (approximately 3%). There are no upfront costs to list your campaign.

How do stretch goals work?

You can set stretch goals at any point during your campaign. We recommend planning them in advance so you can communicate them to backers. Stretch goals are revealed as funding milestones are reached.
